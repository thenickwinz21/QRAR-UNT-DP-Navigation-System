How It Works

QRAR is simple and easy! Just follow these three easy steps:

  1. 1. Scan

    Scan the QR code where you are.

  2. 2. Search

    Enter the floor number and wing letter.

  3. 3. Find!

    Find your room easily using the given directions!

Ready?

Then what?

Once you have scanned the QR code, you will then be prompted to enter your floor number and wing letter. Detailed directions on how to get your designated location will then be shown.
